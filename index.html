About BLUVEA:

BLUVEA Limited is a Strategic Professional Services provider located in Nairobi, Kenya.
BLUVEA combines a strong network of qualified, capable and like-minded professionals with the common ideal of providing high-quality Professional Services solution in a market that has historically been under-catered in this sector.
BLUVEA is an associate Company of RAMARA Ltd. RAMARA Limited is an award winning Designer and Manufacturer of exclusive hardwood Furniture. BLUVEA grew as a company out of RAMARA as a custom design services provider. BLUVEA has since grown and caters for a wide variety of client needs, from interior design to full business case conceptualization and development.
As BLUVEA, we believe in honesty, ethical operations and high integrity, and we provide highly creative services solutions to our clientele. We are able to provide, through our network of professional and other resources, combined capabilities that are unique, fit for purpose and at the cutting edge of solution provision in the region.
While many projects seem effortless, they are created through fine-tuned processes developed and refined by firms such as us after many years ofeducation and experience. With the health, welfare and safety of our clients and consumers utilizing the spaces we create, we work to increase function, efficiency, productivity and aesthetics in residential and commercial spaces.
